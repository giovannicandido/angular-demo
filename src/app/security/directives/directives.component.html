SecIsAuthenticated

Checa se a pessoa está logada

<div *secIsAuthenticated> You are authenticated </div>

Result

You are Authenticated

SecIsHasRole

Verifica se pessoa está em grupo

<div *secHasRole="'president'">
You are The President
<button class="uk-button uk-button-danger">Launch Nuclear Attack</button>
</div>

<div *secHasRole="'admin'">
You are admin
<button class="uk-button uk-button-danger">Reset Password</button>
</div>

Result

You are The President
You are admin

Desabilitando

Desabilita um elemento ao invés de removê-lo

<p>
Are you the President?
<button *secHasRole="'president'; action 'addClass'" class="uk-button uk-button-danger">Launch Nuclear Attack
</button>
</p>
<p>
You are admin?
<button *secHasRole="'admin'; action 'addClass'" class="uk-button uk-button-danger">Reset Password</button>
</p>

Result

Are you the President?

You are admin?
